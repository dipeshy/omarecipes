# Choupsuey

## Noodles

### Ingredients

- Egg noodles or spaghetti
- Oil

### Instructions
1. Boil spaghetti and coat with oil
2. Deep fry


## Sauce

### Ingredients
- Onion             : 1 medium cube
- Red pepper        : 1 cube
- Ginger/garlic     : 1 tbsp
- Chicken           : 400gm

*Vegetables*
- Cabbage           : 1 cup
- Carrots           : 1 medium

*Sauce*
- Salt              : 1 tsp
- White pepper      : 1 tsp
- Sugar             : 1 tbsp
- Ketchup           : 4 tbsp
- Chicken stock     : 1 cup
- Corn starch       : 2 tbsp

### Instructions

1. Fry Onion, pepper
2. Fry ginger garlic
3. Add chicken
4. Fry vegetables
5. Add sauce
